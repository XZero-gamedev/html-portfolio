The Best Movies According to Rafil

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Interstellar

Great Sci-fi movie using concepts of real science to tell a heartfelt story of a father and daughter

The Lion King

A great movie about a prince learning humility after being wrongfully exiled by his uncle who usurped his father

Dragon Ball Z: Bardock Father of Goku

Tells the legendary backstory of Bardock and the origin story of how Goku got sent to earth
